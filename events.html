Limerick has many wonderful and entertaining events perfect for taking your mind off
the stress of life and spending some time with friends and family on weekends. A few of these events include the Limerick Milk Market, and the Christmas markets.

Limerick Milk Market is one of the best and most famous attractions. A great place to grab a delicious snack and meet your friends, it's one of the unique experiences that Limerick has to offer.

The Christmas Market in Limerick is special; it's filled with amazing atmosphere, and lots of great food and activities to enjoy and spend time with family.
